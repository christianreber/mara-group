About this house

This exceptional finca is set in a unique location with breathtaking views of the sea, the bay of Alcudia and the surrounding mountains. It offers privacy just 1 km from the beach.

The ground floor features a spacious entrance hall, open-plan living and dining with open kitchen, pantry, utility room, two double bedrooms, and direct terrace and heated pool access.

Upstairs, four bedrooms each include en suite bathrooms and fantastic sea views. The master suite also features a terrace with mountain views.

The approximately 14,200 m² plot includes landscaped lawns, olive trees, barbecue area and playground. In 2024, a 13.44 kWp photovoltaic system and Tesla storage battery (14 kWh) were installed.
